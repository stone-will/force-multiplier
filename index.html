Why Me

A story of information asymmetry.

After six years in the British Army, I took an MBA and moved into finance. My route wasn't pre-ordained. Before, during, and since, I spoke with dozens of people who had made the transition before me, who generously gave their time to explain what people actually do in the civilian world. Some of these became colleagues, several became friends. And I still call around asking for advice. But I now have my own experience that I can pass on, and I've spoken to dozens of people leaving the armed forces seeking the same advice I benefitted from.

This site is my attempt to pass that on. No jargon, no patronising quick-start guides. Clear answers to the first questions people have: what does a role title mean? What is a realistic option for me? If I take this route, where does it lead? Enough information so that if you do want to speak, we can cut right to the chase. I've never said no to a call.
