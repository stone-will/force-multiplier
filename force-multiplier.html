About This Site

Built by someone who has been there.

After six years in the Army, I took an MBA and moved into finance. Along the way, I benefitted enormously from the generosity of people who had made the transition before me — people who took the time to explain how the civilian world actually works.

This site is my attempt to pass that on. Not a list of resources. Not a government checklist. A genuine map of the landscape, written for officers who are ambitious, capable, and just need someone to explain what's out there.
